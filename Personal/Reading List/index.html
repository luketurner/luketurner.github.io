Books Read

I began maintaining a booklist in June, 2013. These are the books I’ve read since then.

Because I use public transit to commute, I finish books at a rapid clip. Typically I can read a book in a matter of a few days.

But wait — where are all the programming books? I prefer learning about programming online, either via structured courses on a site like Coursera, or by simply reading and researching whatever topic is relevant to me at the time. This is a matter of preference and expense.
